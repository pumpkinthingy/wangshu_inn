The different room tier's

Simple traveler's option

The perfect choice for traveler's on a budget! A simple bedroom with a desk and a chair to document your journey. It's cheap price and accsessability makes it a favourite for traveler's that needs a place to rest for the night and tourists wanting to explore the great landscape surrounding the inn.

Merchant's Mini Suite

The Mini Suite option is the perfect pick for any merchant. Including the suite, you also get help with storage of vares and the option to prepare your own food in your suite. Due to our location, many merchant's have to go travel past our Inn. This has made our Inn a prime location to discuss all the newest mercantile news, and to gather intel

The Liqyue Qixing Suite

A suite fit for the ruling body of Liyue the Qixing. With ample space and the best view, you are assured to have the best possbile experience available in Liyue. This choice also offers the most amount of customization and we will do everything in our power to make this experience to your liking.
